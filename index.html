02 / Projects

Featured work.

Three projects spanning healthcare, motorsport, and financial NLP — collectively demonstrating all six program learning outcomes. Two of them include live interactive demos below.

Patient Feedback Automation System

Capstone · Internship
Loretto Hospital & Rehabilitation Center · Operations Data Analyst
Goals
Design and pilot an automated patient feedback pipeline using DMAIC / Lean Six Sigma methodology — replacing a manual, paper-based review process with a scalable system that surfaces actionable insights for hospital leadership.
Stack
PythonNLPLean Six SigmaSIPOCVSMPower BI
Contribution
Led the DMAIC engagement end-to-end: defined the problem with stakeholders, built SIPOC and current/future-state value stream maps, automated the feedback classification pipeline, and delivered a final pilot report with projected ROI.
Insight
Reduced manual review overhead substantially and gave leadership a near-real-time view of recurring service gaps. The pilot's projected ROI justified a phased rollout.

03 / Track

Data Science track.

Why I chose it, and what it gave me.

I wanted to build models, not just read them.

The Data Science track pushed me into the actual mechanics — ML, NLP, programming, statistics — instead of staying on the dashboard side. That's where the work I want to do lives, and pairing it with my Lean Six Sigma background means I can both build the system and explain why it matters to the business.
